analytics Business Context & Problem

The Electoral Registry faced a massive administrative bottleneck. Each week, they handled roughly 100 manual requests for specific voter statistics from political parties, academic institutions, and internal departments.

The response time averaged 4 business days per request, as staff had to manually query disparate legacy systems and format data into spreadsheets. This lack of accessibility delayed critical political analysis and research across the nation.

The Pain Point

Manual query execution for every single request.

The Opportunity

Democratize data access via central BI platform.
