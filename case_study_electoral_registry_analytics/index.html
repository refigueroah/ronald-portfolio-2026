The Challenge:
Fragmented Data & Manual Latency

Managing a national electoral registry is a high-stakes operation where **accuracy is not optional**. Before this project, the institution faced a critical bottleneck: data was siloed across more than 20 regional branches.

Every time executive leadership required a demographic report or registration update, regional clerks had to manually export spreadsheets. This created a **3-day delay** for critical reports and introduced significant risks of human error and data duplication.

The Solution:
Automated Intelligence Ecosystem

As the Senior Analyst, I architected a solution that moved the institution from reactive to proactive. I engineered a centralized **SQL-based Data Warehouse** that automatically synced with regional databases every night.

By creating a layer of **Power BI dashboards**, I empowered decision-makers to access real-time registration metrics without calling a single regional clerk. This not only streamlined operations but also ensured that national electoral strategies were based on audited, live data.
