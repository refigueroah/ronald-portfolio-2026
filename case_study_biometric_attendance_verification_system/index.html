The Challenge:
Identity Fraud & Manual Friction

In a high-security institutional environment, verifying the physical presence of personnel is critical. The previous system relied on manual paper logs and basic ID cards, which were highly susceptible to **"buddy punching" and identity fraud**.

With over 40,000 users across 40+ national locations, the administrative overhead of auditing these logs was immense. The institution needed a tamper-proof, high-availability solution that could operate 24/7 without constant human supervision.

The Solution:
24/7 Automated Verification

I led the end-to-end implementation of an automated Biometric Kiosk network. We deployed customized hardware units equipped with fingerprint and facial recognition technology, all synced to a secure central database.

By engineering a real-time monitoring dashboard, we provided HR and Security teams with immediate alerts on discrepancies. The system replaced manual intervention with a **fail-safe digital audit trail**, ensuring 100% compliance with institutional security protocols.
