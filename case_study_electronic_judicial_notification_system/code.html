Business Context & Problem

Traditional paper-based notifications across the nationwide court network were causing critical bottlenecks. The manual process resulted in unprecedented operational costs and significant legal delays, as physical couriers and registered mail failed to provide the speed required for modern judicial proceedings.

The Solution

We engineered a unified Web-based Notification Portal that leverages industry-standard encryption and digital signatures. The platform serves as a secure, centralized hub where all judicial notifications are delivered, tracked, and archived instantly.
